About Me

I'm a College Student

I am a student majoring in English Education at the University of Bengkulu. Since beginning my studies, I have grown increasingly fascinated by the role of English as a tool for communication that bridges diverse cultures worldwide. My interest in teaching English has deepened alongside my understanding of how language proficiency can open doors to both professional and personal opportunities.
Throughout my studies, I have focused on mastering various language skills, including speaking, listening, reading, and writing, as well as exploring linguistic theories and effective teaching methodologies. I am particularly drawn to innovations in education, such as leveraging technology to create more interactive and engaging language learning experiences for students.
I am committed not only to achieving academic success but also to making a meaningful contribution to the field of English education. With a passion for learning and a desire for continuous growth, I aspire to enhance the quality of English teaching in Indonesia and help the younger generation master the language in effective and enjoyable ways.
